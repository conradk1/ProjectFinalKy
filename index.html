What I need for a swim meet

  1. Swim Gear
    • Cap
    • Goggles
    • Nose Plug
    • Paddles
    • Kick Board
    • Swim Bag
  2. Snacks
  3. Water
  4. Coach
  5. towels
  6. Suit
  7. Good shoes

People go to thousands of meets a year, and there are many need to know factors that play into a swim meet. They can be anywhere from 2 hours to 6 hours, and multiple events. In a sense a swim meet is much like a track meet. My best advice to the reader is to communicate with your swimmer if you attend one, they will help you naviagte the complicated system of events and heats.
